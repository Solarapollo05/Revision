Bristol

* A UK City in the South West of England
* One of the UK's 10 core cities
* Leading green city
* Located on the M4, an important trade route between London and Wales
* Second largest chip manufacturing industry 
* Large container port

Migration in Bristol:

* Multicultural City - 16% of population in non-white ethnicity
* Migrants were employed where there were gaps in jobs - the economy continued to grow
* The migrants bring their culture to the city - Bristol's entertainment and hospitality industries are world famous
* St Paul's Carnival celebrates the culture of Bristol each year - attracting 40,000 visitors which profits the economy

* However the rapidly increasing population causes problems:
	
	* Housing Shortages
	* Pressure on Healthcare and Education services
